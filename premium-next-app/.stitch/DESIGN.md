# Design System: TechBridge Premium Next App
**Project ID:** local-techbridge

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
A cinematic, deep-dark, glassmorphic aesthetic. The mood is highly technical and premium, relying on deep abyssal backgrounds with brilliant electric/navy blue bioluminescent glows and layered glass surfaces.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Abyss Void (Background)** (#06070A) - Deepest background for the entire application.
*   **Deep Abyss (Surface)** (#0D1015) - Used for elevated surfaces and card backgrounds.
*   **Brilliant Azure (Primary)** (#2563eb) - The primary brand accent, used for active states, primary buttons, and glowing auras.
*   **Ice Azure (Accent Light)** (#93c5fd) - Used for highlights and top-edge radiants on glass surfaces.
*   **Midnight Navy (Accent Dark)** (#0f172a) - Used for subtle, deep shadows and inset glows.
*   **Starlight White (Text Primary)** (#F8FAFC) - High-contrast, crisp text for primary content.
*   **Muted Starlight (Text Secondary)** (#94A3B8) - Used for secondary text and subtle borders.

## 3. Typography Rules
Uses clean, modern sans-serif fonts (likely Inter or similar) with monospace accents (`ui-monospace`) for technical data points or coordinates.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Glass Containers / Shells:** Subtle borders using a mix of 2% white opacity and inset glowing borders, backed by heavy drop shadows (e.g. `0 20px 56px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.28)`) and radial gradient blurs.
*   **Glowing Elements:** Elements cast a soft, wide aura (`0 0 40px rgba(37, 99, 235, 0.35)`).

## 5. Layout Principles
Items float with generous padding on vast dark canvases. Focus is drawn using light gradients and glowing, slow-drifting animations rather than dense borders.
