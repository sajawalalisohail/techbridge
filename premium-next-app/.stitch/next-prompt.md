A premium, cinematic interactive 3D globe section for the About page, featuring subtle rotation and a dark, high-fidelity earth texture with dynamic data arcs connecting Morgantown, WV to Lahore, Pakistan.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark, cinematic, premium
- Background: Abyss Void (#06070A) - absolutely no outer container borders or "pills" containing the globe. Let the globe breathe against the section's background gradients.
- Primary Accent: Brilliant Azure (#2563eb) for glowing points and telemetry
- Accent Light: Ice Azure (#93c5fd) for markers and hover states
- Text Primary: Starlight White (#F8FAFC) for headings
- Text Secondary: Muted Starlight (#94A3B8) for labels and coordinates

**Page Structure:**
1. **Section Header:** Minimal, centered heading (e.g., "Global Reach") in Starlight White.
2. **3D Globe Area:** A large, high-fidelity textured Earth displaying land/sea boundaries. **No outer background pill or borders constraint**.
3. **Data Points:** Two glowing markers representing Morgantown, WV and Lahore, Pakistan.
4. **Location Labels:** Small, highly legible typography (10-12px) attached directly to the coordinate points. **Do not use pill tags or background boxes for labels.** Use subtle drop shadows for legibility.
5. **Data Arcs:** Highly visible, glowing traveling data particles arching over the globe surface between the two points to simulate live telemetry.
6. **Interactive Legend:** A floating glassmorphic card displaying network status overlaid on the top right.
